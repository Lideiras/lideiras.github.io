    My Role: I was responsible for implementing the bow and arrow mechanics, including physics-based arrow trajectory, different arrow types with unique effects, and a satisfying draw-and-release system. I also contributed to level design, creating environments that encourage parkour movement while providing strategic combat opportunities.
